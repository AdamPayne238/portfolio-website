Lambda School - June 2019 to Present

As a student at Lambda School I am expected to learn new concepts and implement them in a project every day to grow as a developer. Every Friday we have a three hour evaluation of our current understanding of the concepts we learned over the week, and every fourth week we implement our understanding in a week long "Build Week" where we get a chance to collaborate with other students on a larger scale project.
